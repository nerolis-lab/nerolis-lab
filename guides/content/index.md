Welcome to Neroli's Guides, a collaboration between Neroli's Lab and the Route 01 aides in the [Pokémon Sleep discord server](https://discord.gg/pokemonsleep). This wiki is a comprehensive strategy resource for Pokémon Sleep, covering progression, team building, Pokémon evaluation, resource management, game mechanics, and more. Whether you just started or you're deep into the endgame, the guides here are regularly updated to reflect the current state of the game.

## Subjective Content

Not everything in this wiki is objective fact; Some pages describe game mechanics, while others share the community's strategic recommendations based on analysis and experience. To keep this distinction clear, any page containing opinion or strategic advice begins with this banner:
<SubjectiveInfoBanner />
This way, you always know whether you're reading how the game works or how we think it's best played.

## Contributing

This wiki is a community effort, and contributions are welcome.
